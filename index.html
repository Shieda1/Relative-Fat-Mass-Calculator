What is the relative fat mass calculator?
The relative fat mass calculator is a tool that predicts body fat mass.
How to calculate relative fat mass?
To calculate relative fat mass you should use the following formula:
relative fat mass (men) = 64 - (20 * height / WC)
relative fat mass (women) = 76 - (20 * height /WC)
